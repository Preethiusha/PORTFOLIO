WEATHER PREDICTION USING ML

ABSTRACT:

The development and deployment of a machine learning (ML) model for weather prediction, utilizing a combination of algorithms including K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), Logistic Regression, and Decision Tree. Through rigorous experimentation, the Decision Tree algorithm emerged as the most accurate predictor for weather forecasting. Leveraging the trained dataset, a web application was designed and implemented to disseminate weather information for the upcoming five days. The application harnesses the predictive power of the Decision Tree model to offer users reliable forecasts. This abstract highlights the significance of employing ML techniques, specifically the Decision Tree algorithm, in real-world applications such as weather prediction, enhancing accessibility and accuracy of weather forecasts through user-friendly interfaces.
